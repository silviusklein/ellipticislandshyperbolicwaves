From elliptic islands to hyperbolic waves

Celebrating Pedro Duarte's 60th birthday



This event, consisting of an international conference on Dynamical Systems and a special edition of the EDAÍ seminar, celebrates Pedro Duarte's 60th birthday by focusing on his research interests.

The main topics covered in this meeting are: the standard map with its challenges, including the existence of elliptic islands; hyperbolic billiards; isospectral reduction theory; statistical properties of linear cocycles showing hyperbolicity waves, as well as positivity and continuity of their Lyapunov exponents; the study of ergodic Schrödinger operators in mathematical physics.

The (spectral) gap between the two-day conference at the beginning of the week and the one-day EDAÍ seminar (a traditional carioca event in dynamics) at the end of the week, will allow some of the speakers or participants to collaborate with local researchers.
